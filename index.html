About Me

Greetings! I'm Sunita Jaiswal, a certified image consultant and the founder behind Style Solutions Consulting, my brainchild project dedicated to helping you uncover and refine your unique personal style.

My mission is to create a comfortable, secure environment where we collaborate to achieve your style aspirations. With specialized expertise in image consulting, I guide individuals in discovering their personal style, understanding their body type, and finding their perfect color season. Together, we'll curate your personal style that resonates confidence in every aspect of your life.

Beyond image consulting, I am deeply passionate about emotionally supporting individuals on their journey of personal growth, nurturing their confidence, and guiding them towards finding their own happiness.

Thank you for entrusting me with the journey towards your enhanced personal style. Let’s embark on this transformation together!
